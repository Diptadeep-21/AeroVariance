
# AeroVariance — AI-Powered Urban Air Quality Intelligence for Smart City Intervention

> **Hackathon Theme:** Smart Cities · Environmental Intelligence · Geospatial Analytics · Public Health

## Executive Summary
AeroVariance is an AI-powered Urban Air Quality Intelligence platform that transforms environmental monitoring into actionable intelligence. It combines real-time AQI monitoring, AI forecasting, explainable machine learning, multilingual advisories, geospatial insights, and decision support for city administrators.

## Problem Statement Alignment
India has extensive AQI monitoring infrastructure but lacks an intelligence layer for proactive interventions. AeroVariance addresses this by delivering:
- Pollution source attribution
- Hyperlocal AQI forecasting
- Enforcement intelligence
- Citizen health advisories
- Multi-city analytics

## Solution Overview
The platform integrates monitoring data, weather information, AI prediction, SHAP explainability, analytics, and multilingual communication to help authorities make evidence-based decisions.

## Core Features
- Real-time AQI Dashboard
- Hyperlocal AQI Prediction
- Explainable AI (SHAP)
- Pollution Source Attribution
- Citizen Health Advisory
- Analytics Dashboard
- Intervention Simulation
- Multi-city Comparison
- Multilingual Support

## Unique Value Proposition
Unlike conventional dashboards, AeroVariance predicts future AQI, explains predictions, recommends interventions, and communicates risks in regional languages.
